April is trained in Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR; trauma focused therapy), Gottman Trained for Couples (Level 1), utilizes Redecision Therapy and has a theological orientation consisting of both Cognitive Behavioral Therapy and Client Centered Therapy. April has 20 years of mental health experience (one to one behavioral counseling, intensive in-home therapy, therapeutic foster care supervision and licensing preparation, group therapy and individual outpatient therapy).

April serves adolescents, adults and couples both in office and telehealth. She has worked in a variety of settings including group practice, homes, schools, residential facilities and hospitals. She specializes in helping adolescents and adults struggling with mental health symptoms such as depression, trauma, anxiety, anger, social/relationship problems, difficulty with communication, mood disturbances, self-esteem and personal identity.

April believes in a strong, authentic therapeutic alliance between client and therapist and is committed to helping each individual overcome his/her struggles to accomplish personal goals. April is a seasoned therapist who also serves as the Clinical Director of The Kohler Group, PLLC providing supervision to our clinicians as well as counseling students we are training to eventually become Christian Counselors. In her free time, April is known and respected for her beautiful singing voice and keyboard skills which she uses as a worship leader. She is also a certified personal trainer and is completing her training to be a Financial Peace University financial coach.
