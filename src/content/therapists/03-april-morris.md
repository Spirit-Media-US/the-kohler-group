April Morris serves as Clinical Director of The Kohler Group, PLLC. As a Licensed Clinical Mental Health Counselor Supervisor (LCMHCS) and Nationally Certified Counselor (NCC), April brings a wealth of clinical expertise and a genuine passion for helping clients grow in their faith and find new perspectives. She is also an approved supervisor for counselors seeking licensure in North Carolina.
