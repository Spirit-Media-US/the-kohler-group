Barbara has over 28 years professional counseling experience in several different venues. She has ministered in Eastern US from New York to Florida as well as Italy and Greece. You can hear Barbara's recording project *Through the Eyes of the Father* on [Apple Music / iTunes](https://music.apple.com).

Barbara is licensed to provide psychotherapy in North and South Carolina. She sees clients in our office in Huntersville, NC as well as provides telehealth to NC and SC residents. Barbara provides therapy intensives for individuals and married couples to help clients resolve trauma, forgive others, ask forgiveness and enjoy the Christ centered life. You can read about the therapy intensives [here](/intensives/individual).
