Barbara J. Kohler is the founder of The Kohler Group, PLLC. She holds a Master of Science degree and is a Certified Rehabilitation Counselor (CRC), Licensed Clinical Mental Health Counselor (LCMHC), and Nationally Certified Counselor (NCC). Barbara is known for her work in marriage intensives and her deep commitment to helping individuals and couples find healing through Biblical principles and evidence-based clinical practice.
